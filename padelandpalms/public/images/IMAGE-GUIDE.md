# Image Guide for Padel & Palms Website

Place your images in this directory (`public/images/`) with the following filenames:

## Required Images

### 1. **hero-background.jpg**
- **Usage:** Hero section background (full-screen)
- **Recommended Size:** 1920x1080px or larger
- **Description:** Main hero image for the Padel & Palms landing section. Should show your padel courts, tropical setting, or overall venue
- **Location in App:** Panel ID: "hero" (Line 166 in App.tsx)

---

### 2. **booking-phone.jpg**
- **Usage:** Panel 1 - The Booking Magic section
- **Recommended Size:** 800x600px
- **Description:** Image showing someone booking on their phone, ideally in a tropical resort setting or at your venue
- **Location in App:** Panel ID: "panel1" (Line 214 in App.tsx)

---

### 3. **villa-exterior.jpg**
- **Usage:** Panel 3 - Global Calendar Integrity section
- **Recommended Size:** 800x600px
- **Description:** Exterior shot of your luxury tropical villa or accommodation
- **Location in App:** Panel ID: "panel3" (Line 289 in App.tsx)

---

### 4. **coconut-drink.jpg**
- **Usage:** Panel 4 - WhatsApp F&B Upsell section
- **Recommended Size:** 800x400px
- **Description:** Fresh coconut drink or beverage offerings from your F&B menu
- **Location in App:** Panel ID: "panel4" (Line 335 in App.tsx)

---

### 5. **padel-action.jpg**
- **Usage:** Panel 6 - Brand Engagement section
- **Recommended Size:** 800x600px
- **Description:** Dynamic action shot of padel being played at your courts
- **Location in App:** Panel ID: "panel6" (Line 449 in App.tsx)

---

### 6. **morning-coffee.jpg**
- **Usage:** Panel 7 - Daily Pulse Dashboard section
- **Recommended Size:** 800x600px
- **Description:** Morning coffee scene with view of tropical landscape or your venue
- **Location in App:** Panel ID: "panel7" (Line 502 in App.tsx)

---

## Image Format Tips

- **Formats:** JPG, JPEG, PNG, or WEBP
- **Optimization:** Compress images to reduce file size while maintaining quality (use tools like TinyPNG)
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Hero: 16:9 (landscape)
  - Comic images: 4:3 or 3:2 (landscape)
  - Coconut drink: 2:1 (wide landscape)

---

## Quick Start

1. Rename your images to match the filenames above
2. Copy all 6 images to this `public/images/` directory
3. Refresh your browser at http://localhost:3000
4. All images should now display correctly!

---

## Troubleshooting

If images don't appear:
- Check that filenames match exactly (case-sensitive on some systems)
- Ensure images are in the `public/images/` folder
- Hard refresh your browser (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
- Check browser console for any errors
